About Me

I was born on a ship-wrecked island off the coast of northern Japan. Sounds interesting, but now that I have your attention, the first sentence is not true! I was born and raised in a desert country in the Middle East (Kuwait). Born of Indian parents, I learned very early life to be open to different cultures and languages. I know four langauges and am fluent in three of them. I've lived in Kuwait, India, UK and have now settled down in the US.

I have a Bachelors in English Literature and a Masters Degree in Business. I started my career at a start-up company that had launched the first ever e-commerce website in the Middle East called kunoozy.com (meaning treasure). After a few years, and several organizations later in Oct 2010, I started work at my first ever true IT organization - UPS. I enjoy working here, the culture is great, and I have enough challenges to keep me motivated.

In other personal news - I love watching movies, reading books, walking, and hiking - a list of your typical run-of-the-mill things that most people like. I also enjoy photography and image processing as a hobby and at some younger point in life, was passionate about horse-back riding.
